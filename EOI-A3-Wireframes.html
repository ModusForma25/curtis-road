Modus Forma + TCL · Curtis Road LXR · EOI to Acciona

A3 Layout Wireframes — 10-Page Proposal Document

Landscape A3 (420 × 297 mm), single pages, reading as sequential spreads. One hero element per page at 50–60% of the sheet, body text locked to 3-column measures for legibility, and exactly one “loud” moment in the whole document (the reversed metrics panel, Sheet 04). Proportions are exact; screen scale is indicative.

Imagery rules

  • Full bleed only on cover, back cover and the Sheet 03 section drawing — everything else locks to the grid.
  • Every image earns a working caption (7.5 pt) that carries an argument, not a description.
  • Renders from the concept model are the hero currency — treat photos as support at 3–4 col sizes.
  • Diagrams share one line-weight + colour system across all 10 pages.

Colour & rhythm

  • Neutral page; one accent drawn from the render palette, used for eyebrows, keylines, relevance lines.
  • Tinted panels ≤ 10% for context/callouts; one reversed panel in the whole document (Sheet 04 metrics).
  • Win-theme eyebrow + folio in the same position on every page — the document’s heartbeat.
  • Hero : text ratio ≈ 60 : 40 on analysis pages, 50 : 50 on evidence pages.

Layout rationale

  1. One loop diagram, not four small ones.Catchment → treatment → irrigation → canopy as a single continuous drawing: the argument is circularity, so the diagram should literally close.
  2. Four strategy blocks in a strict 2 × 2.Each labelled with its owner (MF / TCL / BOTH) — the eyebrow does the attribution so the body text doesn’t have to.
  3. Metrics reversed, full height, tabular numerals.Six stat tiles (carbon ↓, tonnes ↓, canopy ↑, habitat ↑, species ↑, stormwater ↑), each with its concept-design baseline. Being the sole dark object in 10 pages, it becomes the page Acciona photographs and reuses in their own tender.
